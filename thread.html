04 Thread Magazine

As art director, I led the layout design, created graphics for and edited all photos for the SS18 and FW18 issues of Thread Magazine. The SS18 issue focuses on technology and its impact on fashion and our lives while the FW18 issues closes in on college students' ideologies and cultures through stereotypical yearbook motifs.
