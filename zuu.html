Rebranding

The startup's main focus is to educated consumers on the fintech market and encourage stakeholders to wisely invest. ZUU understands that finance can be difficult and possibly intimidating for the layman user; thus, in rebranding, I wanted to reflect upon this goal and give ZUU a professional but approachable look.

The startup had already established the two red shades below as company colors before I came. However, when I looked at all their old designs, I thought that the use of only white as a background color made for good contrast, but seemed a little bit plain. Thus, I introduced the two shades of grey-blue to offset this harshness and make company material appear more modern.
