01 GoDaddy Internship

Many users suggested that changing visual aspects of their website such as theme, color and font were difficult. With this prototype, I separated the three elements so that users would not have to go through the whole theme-changing process to change just one elements. After using testing my prototype, I discovered that dividing the processes of changing theme, color and font into separate experiences was effective. However, the current process for changing color and accent color was still difficult. These results helped to corroborate the decision to completely transform the color picking process and led to the real implementation of the theme picking experience I designed.
